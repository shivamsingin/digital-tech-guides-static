What is Local SEO?

Local SEO is the practice of optimizing your online presence to attract more business from relevant local searches. It's about making your business visible when people search for products or services in your area.

When someone searches for "restaurants near me" or "plumber in [city]", Local SEO ensures your business appears in the local search results, Google Maps, and other local directories.
